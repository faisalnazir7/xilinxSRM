Welcome to xilinxNexus SRM


"Towards Technical Empowerment"

Xilinx NEXUS is an innovative programme of SRMIST from the grants received from Xilinx, Inc., focused on the funding of Women in Technology (WIT) programmes within the university setting.

Vision of Xilinx NEXUS

The goal of the prgram is to increase the representation, participation and entrepreneurial skills and abilities of women in Technology focused careers.


MISSION of Xilinx NEXUS

  • Educate Students through STEM programmes to integrate them into a cohesive learning paradigm based on real world applications.



  • Develop new skill sets through mentorship events through seminars and workshops.



  • Steer girl students in the direction of the research and development for the benefit of women folk.
