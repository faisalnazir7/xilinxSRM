About



Xilinx NEXUS is an innovative program of SRMIST from the grants
received from Xilinx, Inc., focused on the funding of Women in
Technology(WIT) programs within the university setting.






Vision of Xilinx NEXUS



The goal of the prgram is to increase the representation, participation and entrepreneurial skills and abilities of women in Technology focused careers.







MISSION of Xilinx NEXUS is to:



  • Educate Students through STEM programs to integrate them into a cohesive learning paradigm based on real world applications.



  • Develop new skill sets through mentorship events through seminars and workshops.



  • Steer girl students in the direction of the research and development for the benefit of women folk.
